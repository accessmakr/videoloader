Methodology & Processing Logic

VideoLoader utilizes the robust Cobalt API architecture, a privacy-centric media processing engine. Unlike traditional downloaders that inject intrusive ads and tracking cookies, our methodology centers on stateless requests.

  • Tunneling Technology: We securely tunnel requests to bypass regional restrictions while maintaining your anonymity.
  • Dynamic Parsing: Our logic identifies whether the source is a single video, a gallery, or a GIF, providing a "Media Picker" for complex URLs.
  • Client-Side Processing: The interface handles state management locally for near-zero latency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is VideoLoader free to use? ↓

Yes, VideoLoader is 100% free with no hidden subscriptions or premium tiers. We believe in open access to web tools.

Which platforms are supported? ↓

We support most major video-sharing platforms and social media networks. If a link is public, VideoLoader can likely process it.

Can I download 4K videos? ↓

Absolutely. By selecting 'Max' quality, our engine retrieves the highest available resolution provided by the source platform.

Is my data stored on your servers? ↓

No. VideoLoader operates on a strict zero-log policy. We do not store URLs, user data, or downloaded media.

Why did my download fail? ↓

Downloads may fail if the video is private, age-restricted, or if the source platform has updated its security protocols. Try a different URL.

Can I convert video to MP3? ↓

Yes. Toggle the 'Extract Audio Only' checkbox before clicking 'Go' to receive a high-quality MP3 file.

Is there a limit on file size? ↓

There is no hard limit from our side, though extremely large files may be limited by your browser's memory or the API provider's bandwidth.

Do I need to install any software? ↓

No. VideoLoader is a fully web-based application that works directly in your browser on iOS, Android, Windows, and macOS.
