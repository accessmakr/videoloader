Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

VideoLoader (intelreap.com) is a free, web-based video downloader and MP3 converter. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains that we collect, store, or share zero personal data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No URLs, video titles, or download history
  • No IP addresses or device information stored
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No user accounts or email collection
  • No analytics or third-party tracking tools

Every request is completely stateless and processed through public Cobalt instances (co.wuk.sh and api.cobalt.tools). Once the download link is generated, we discard everything.

3. Local Storage (Dark Mode Only)

We use browser localStorage solely to remember your dark/light mode preference. This data never leaves your device and contains no personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely exclusively on public Cobalt API endpoints. These services process the video link you provide on-the-fly and return a direct download link. They do not receive any data from us, and we do not receive any data from them beyond the temporary response.

5. Your Rights

Because we store nothing, there is nothing to delete or export. You have full control — simply close the tab and your activity disappears instantly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this page occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with an updated date. Last updated: March 15, 2026.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, please use the Contact page or email us at privacy@intelreap.com (we reply within 48 hours).
